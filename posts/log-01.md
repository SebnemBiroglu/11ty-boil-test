![Earth as a tiny speck in a sunbeam](/images/palebluedot.png)

**TIMESTAMP:** 2026-03-01 // 10:36 UTC  
**COORDINATES:** 6.4 Billion Kilometers from Earth  

At the request of the project lead, the *Astraeus* executed a 180-degree yaw to capture a final sequence of the inner solar system. 

The resulting image shows Earth as a single pixel of light, approximately 0.12 pixels in size. It is suspended in a scattered light beam resulting from the camera's optics. From this vantage point, the planetary surface is indistinguishable. There is no evidence of the biosphere or human infrastructure. 

**ANALYSIS:** Total planetary isolation confirmed.
