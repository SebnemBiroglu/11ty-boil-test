**TIMESTAMP:** 2026-03-15 // 04:12 UTC  
**TARGET:** Messier 16 [The Eagle Nebula]

The *Astraeus* has successfully oriented its spectrograph toward the three columns of cold interstellar hydrogen and dust. 

**OBSERVATIONS:**
1. **Photoevaporation:** The tops of the pillars are being illuminated and eroded by the ultraviolet light from the massive stars in the cluster NGC 6611.
2. **EGGs:** Evaporating Gaseous Globules are visible at the fringes. These are regions of high-density gas where star formation is likely occurring.
3. **Radiation:** Background radiation levels have spiked to 140 mSv. Shielding is holding.

**CONCLUSION:** Star nursery activity is consistent with 1995 Hubble baseline data.
